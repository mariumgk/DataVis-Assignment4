Conclusion

This assignment explored two datasets under the unifying theme of structure and connectivity — one internal (organizational), one global (geographic). The visualizations collectively reveal:

  • Production dominates the HR hierarchy — ~60% of headcount — yet IT/IS commands the highest average salary, visible in the treemap's colour gradient.
  • The dendrogram confirms departmental cohesion — IT/IS positions cluster tightly (similar salary and satisfaction), while Production spreads widely (high diversity).
  • The route network is scale-free — a small number of hub airports connect the majority of routes, consistent with Barabási–Albert power-law behaviour.
  • Layout choice matters — spring layout surfaces hubs organically, while circular layout makes the tier hierarchy immediately legible. Both views are necessary for complete understanding.
